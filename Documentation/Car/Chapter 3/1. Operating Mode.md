## 1. Operating Mode
### Our car has several operating modes: manual and automatic. To protect the car, we also introduced a new state that we called emergency. This state corresponds to an emergency situation from which the car can only exit when it receives the "end emergency" command from the server.
### When the car is turned on, it will start in automatic mode and in the emergency state. To be able to exit the emergency state, the "end emergency" command must be explicitly sent.  
![Emergency Operating Mode](/Documentation/Car/Images/emergency_mode.png)

### The first operating mode is the autonomous one. When the car is in this mode, it will follow the black lines of the track and will detect the presence of obstacles or the traffic light (via the ultrasonic sensors mounted on the car). If the car detects the traffic light, then the messages published by the traffic light on the "test/masini" topic (which will be published at every state change of the traffic light) will be taken into account. Thus, the car will know what state it is in and will make decisions based on the color indicated by the traffic light.
![Autonomous Operating Mode](/Documentation/Car/Images/autonomous_mode.png)

### The second operating mode is the manual one. In this operating mode, the car will receive commands from the server for the direction of movement. For this, a single transmitted command is necessary because it retains the value of the last command sent and will execute it until it receives a new command (to change direction or enter the emergency state). If the car enters the emergency state, when it exits this state, it will no longer remember what command was given before entering this state.
![Manual Operating Mode](/Documentation/Car/Images/manual_mode.png)
