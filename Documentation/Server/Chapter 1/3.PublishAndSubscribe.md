## 3. Publish/Subscribe

The Publish/Subscribe model has two basic components:

### 1. Publisher
Publishers are the entities that generate and transmit messages. Each message is associated with a specific "topic," which we will explain later. In the context of the MQTT protocol, clients act as publishers when they send messages to the broker.

### 2. Subscribers
Subscribers are the entities that express their interest in receiving certain messages by subscribing to one or more topics. After subscribing, they receive messages published on those specific topics. In MQTT, clients become subscribers when they establish a connection with the broker and specify the topic of interest.
